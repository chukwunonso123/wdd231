Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership options and benefits.

Can I change my membership level after signing up?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. The prorated difference will be charged for the remainder of your term. Downgrades take effect at renewal.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

No, there's no minimum commitment. Paid memberships are for one year and can be canceled anytime, but refunds are not provided for unused portions.

Do I need to own an e-bike to join?

Not at all! Many members join before purchasing to learn more and get advice. We also occasionally have demo events where you can try different models.

How do the exclusive group rides work?

Standard and Premium members get access to special group rides with limited spots. These are announced in advance, and members can register through their account.

What happens if I need to cancel my membership?

You can cancel anytime through your account settings. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.
