Community Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect and kindness. We're a diverse community with different experience levels and interests.

Constructive Communication

Keep discussions constructive, even when disagreeing. Provide helpful feedback and avoid negative comments.

Safety First

Always prioritize safety in discussions and advice. Don't recommend modifications or practices that could be unsafe.

Share Knowledge

Our community thrives when members share their knowledge and experiences. Help others when you can!

Have Fun

This community is about sharing our passion for e-bikes and enjoying our rides. Let's have fun together!
